# LinkedIn Posts

This folder contains manually collected LinkedIn posts from selected B2B marketing and SaaS experts.

LinkedIn was collected manually because public scraping and API access are limited. I selected posts that connect to the research topic through positioning, demand generation, AI visibility, content quality, or B2B content systems.

## Selection Criteria

Posts were included if they helped explain one of the following:

- how B2B SaaS companies should communicate their point of view
- how content quality changes when AI increases content volume
- how positioning affects content strategy
- how marketing teams should use feedback loops
- how buyers and AI systems categorize B2B brands
