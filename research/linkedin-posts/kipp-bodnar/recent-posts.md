### Full Post Text

AI collapsed the buyer’s journey and made the old playbook obsolete. Teams need to move faster than ever to meet the moment. That’s why HubSpot moved away from the traditional funnel and built a marketing Loop.

Here’s the old way: Marketers plan campaigns, execute, and wait to measure performance. By the time teams unlock learnings, the market has already moved.

With Loop Marketing, teams run experiments constantly. They build systems that surface signals in real-time to adapt while campaigns run. If a message isn’t perfect, teams course correct. Learnings happen so fast that setbacks never become full-blown failures.

Teams that bake evolution into their campaigns outpace the linear approach. Learnings compound, and larger strategies can be optimized continuously. That’s the competitive moat most CMOs aren't building yet.

The brands that win have systems that let them learn and adapt.
