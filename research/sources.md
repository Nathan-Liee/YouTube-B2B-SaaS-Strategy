## Selection Criteria

1. **Practical relevance:** selected sources actively create, lead, advise, or operate in B2B content/marketing contexts.
2. **Public material available:** each source has public YouTube videos, interviews, or LinkedIn content that can be reviewed.
3. **B2B SaaS fit:** sources cover positioning, demand generation, content strategy, audience building, GTM, or B2B video/media.
4. **Varied perspectives:** the list includes founders, CMOs, consultants, creators, agencies, and B2B media operators.
5. **Playbook value:** the material is practical enough to support a future YouTube content strategy playbook.
